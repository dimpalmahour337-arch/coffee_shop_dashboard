# coffee_shop_dashboard
<br>
1. Coffee Shop Insights & Product Dashboard
<br>
2. Developed an interactive Power BI Coffee Shop Insights & Product Dashboard to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product profitability. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into key business metrics, including total revenue, total orders, average order value, top-selling products, category-wise sales, and customer trends.
<br>

<br>
  
Implemented data cleaning and transformation using Power Query, created  and designed visually engaging reports with KPIs, charts, slicers, and drill-through functionality. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify high-performing products, track seasonal sales trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve operational efficiency and profitability.
<br>
Key Features:
<br>
• Sales Performance Analysis (Revenue, Orders, Profit Trends)
<br>
• Top-Selling Products & Category Insights
<br>
• Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis
<br>
• Monthly & Daily Sales Trend Tracking
<br>
• Interactive Filters and Slicers
<br>
• KPI Cards for Business Monitoring
<br>
• Dynamic DAX Measures and Calculations
<br>
• Clean and User-Friendly Dashboard Design
<br>

Tools & Technologies: Power BI, Power Query,  Data Modeling, Data Visualization
<br>

Business Impact: Enabled data-driven decision-making by transforming raw sales data into actionable insights, helping identify growth opportunities, optimize product offerings, and improve overall business performance.
